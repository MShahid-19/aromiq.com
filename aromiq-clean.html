Our Promise

Why Coffee Lovers
Choose Aromiq

🌱

Direct from Estate

No middlemen. Sourced directly from Coorg & Chikmagalur farmers — fresher beans, better taste, fairer prices for the people who grow them.

🤲

Hand-Selected Beans

Only grade-A cherries make it into our products. Our team personally visits each harvest — we reject anything that doesn't meet our exact standard.

🔥

Small-Batch Roasted

Never mass-roasted. Every batch is slow-roasted to unlock complex aromas. The difference in your cup is immediate and unmistakable.

📦

Sealed Fresh in Glass

Packed in airtight glass jars within hours of roasting. Every jar you open should smell like it was roasted this very morning.

🚚

Pan India Delivery

Shipped via Delhivery, Shiprocket & DTDC to every corner of India. Typically delivered within 3–5 business days anywhere in the country.

💬

Personal Service Always

Every order is handled by us personally. Questions, custom orders, corporate gifting — message us on WhatsApp and get a real reply fast.
